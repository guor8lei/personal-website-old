About Me

Hi, I'm Raymond! I am a freshman at UC Berkeley majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS). I'm also interested in computational biology and genetics.


I'm proficient in Python and Java, being a Oracle Certified Associate, Java SE 8 Programmer. I'm also familiar with shell scripting, as well as web development, using frameworks such as Django, query languages like GraphQL, and JavaScript libraries including React. This summer, I'll be interning as a system administrator intern at Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory. Over the past few years, I have gained work experience by interning at the National Institutes of Health, University of Maryland, College Park, as well as a startup company, Bidbox, LLC. I also wrote a published research paper, became a Regeneron Science Talent Search (STS) semifinalist, and coded an Android app.


Outside of academics, I love speed-solving the Rubik's Cube, watching movies, and playing ice hockey. In my spare time, I maintain a small blog, where I post my movie reviews.
