About Me

Hi, I'm Raymond! I am a freshman at UC Berkeley majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) and Economics.


I'm proficient in Java, having learned it all throughout high school. I am a Oracle Certified Associate, Java SE 8 Programmer, and I'm currently learning Python, HTML, CSS, and Javascript at Berkeley. Over the past few years, I have gained work experience by interning at the National Institutes of Health, University of Maryland, College Park, as well as a startup company, Bidbox, LLC. I also wrote a published research paper, became a Regeneron Science Talent Search (STS) semifinalist, and coded an Android app.


Outside of academics, I love speed-solving the Rubik's Cube, watching movies, and playing ice hockey. In my spare time, I maintain a small blog, where I post my movie reviews.
