About Me

Hi, I'm Raymond! I'm a junior at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences with a minor in Bioengineering.


This summer, I'm a software engineering intern at Lucid Software. I am currently also a project manager for Berkeley CodeBase, a software consulting organization that builds products for high-growth Bay Area startups.


Previously, I was an undergraduate student researcher in the Molecular Cell Biomechanics Laboratory at UC Berkeley. I was also a network engineer intern at the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory.


I'm proficient in Python and Java. I've worked with web development technologies such as React/Angular for frontend and Django/Rails for backend, as well as other frameworks and libraries.


In my free time, I love watching movies, listening to music, and playing ice hockey. I occasionally write my thoughts on movies and short films in a blog.
